Why this exists

Independent restaurants don't fail because the food is bad. They fail because the loyalty isn't real — and the cash never lasts.

80%
Close within five years.

Points programs don't build loyalty. Discounts erode margins. Your best regulars love you — but nothing keeps them when the next hot opening lands across the street.

90d
The runway between rent and a bad week.

Banks won't lend without collateral. Investors want equity. GoFundMe feels desperate. Your community wants to back you. There's never been a mechanism to let them.

What Rainback does
We turn your most loyal customers into named, numbered members of your restaurant — with real perks, real status, and real cash that arrives the day they join.
